Mission Statement

To promote the exploration and respect of nature and what it has to offer through training, immersion trips, and workshops. POA is committed to the education, treatment, and rehabilitation of nature.


About

Pitzer Outdoor Adventure (POA) is a student-operated organization that serves students at the Claremont Colleges. The main function of POA is providing a forum for students to schedule trips that explore interesting areas in Southern California and beyond. POA meetings are held every Wednesday evening at the Grove House on Pitzer's campus.
